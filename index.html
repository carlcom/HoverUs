History

It started March 5th 2014 when I saw the HUVr Tech Hoax and thought, Why not?

There are many how-to leaf blower hovercraft videos like this from 2010:

I watched and researched but couldn't find what I wanted. Several designs have come up since then, like Hendo (Magnetic Field ArchitectureTM) and Lexus (liquid nitrogen cooled superconductors), but nothing like Hover Us.

I started with an oval board shape and began many years of experimenting, designing, building, and testing.

Finding Seam Grip has allowed me great design flexibility, it works wonders.

An image of the hand drawn skirt profile (used for layout measures), might be a NFT auctioning on OpenSea.

How much weight can it lift? If CFM was the important blower measure, something like the Lasko Wind Machine with 4750 CFM would be awesome. But more pressure is needed. The force on a surface, the ground area under the skirt, is easy to determine with the area and pressure. The pressure of your blower can be measured with a manometer, which is easy to make. You can sort of calculate air pressure with the published speed rating and Bernoulli's equation. I have been developing corporate websites (full-stack ASP.NET) but last year (OMG it's been too long) I decided to focus on Hover Us. Special thanks to someone for her incredible support through this time.

The design has seen many revisions and went from a bag skirt, to wall skirt, to bag and finger, but the current version is crazy. There will be more details, hopefully sooner :)   There is a lot of work to build one, but it is not rocket science. I decided to get some feedback and make improvements, especially making the build easier, before taking orders. We will also release a free how-to STEM focused PDF that covers detailed steps to design and build your own. The Hover Us design and material kits will also be available.

FAQs

Q: Will it work on water?
A: Unknown and you should not try it. It might be as buoyant as 2 body boards with 20 pounds (9Kg) on top, but hard to balance.
Q: What will it work on?
A: On smoother surfaces it works great. On typical street, sidewalk, and other hard surfaces it does well. Offroad testing is pending.
Q: What is the range?
A: It's not designed for transportation, just to have fun. It will run about 10 minutes on a charge.
Q: When does KickStarter launch? How can I find out more?
A: Unfortunately this has been more difficult than I thought possible. I will make the design open and free. Follow Hover Us and check back.
Q: How about multiple leaf blowers?
A: Using 2 means both pressure and flow with the Glove bag.
